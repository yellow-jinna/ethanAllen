Developers • Design firms • Contractors • Purchasing agents • Iconic brands

For nearly a century, we’ve created interior design experiences that exceed expectations. Now, our innovative designers and product creators are reinventing the way hospitality environments are created everywhere.

When Only The Best Will Do

  • From Luxe Lobbies To Posh Patios

    Indoor and outdoor furnishings, indoors and outdoor fabrics, lighting, rugs and carpeting, wallcoverings, bedding, artwork, and accessories—if you need it, we can craft it or source it quickly from our well-established network of top-tier vendors.

  • One Place For Everything

    Work with a single vendor from design concept to manufacturing to installation—no more complex webs of suppliers. Working with an additional design firm? No problem. We’ll work to coordinate our finishes and materials with theirs.

  • Quality & Customization

    Personalize everything in the styles, fabrics, and finishes that burnish your brand image, backed by the extraordinary quality and craftsmanship for which Ethan Allen is so well known.

  • Socially Responsible & Sustainable

    From championing fair labor standards for artisans to responsibly sourcing wood from Appalachian forests, we’re committed to a future that preserves our natural resources while ensuring dignity and justice for artisans all over the world.

  • Complete Line Of Products

    Case • Upholstery • Outdoor furniture • Lighting • Floorcoverings • Wallcoverings • Mattresses and bedding • Throws and decorative pillows • Artwork and mirrors • Clocks • Florals and trees • Sculpture and accents • Window treatments

  • Exemplary Concierge Service

    Every project gets a dedicated project manager. One phone call can get you the status of your lobby sofas, guest room dressers, restaurant tables and chairs, and the vases you’d like in the spa. If changes arise, you’ll hear from us immediately.
