  • Expansive Product Program

    With nearly 75% of our offerings crafted in our own North American workshops, and our vertically integrated business structure, we can control the process from start to finish, which gives us the advantage when it comes to quality and deliverability.

  • Access To A Global Marketing Team

    Our dedicated web, eCommerce, design, writing, and production teams work to position Ethan Allen as the most desired aspirational brand by creating a strong branded presence online, on TV, through digital and printed pieces, and via email, social media, and PR.

  • Advanced Technology At The Ready

    Clients and associates alike benefit from our powerfully collaborative design tools. These include our proprietary 3D Room Planner, EA InHome App®, Rug Visualizer, Custom Frame Studio, and more! Designers also have access to our online POS system.

  • Support For New Store Openings

    New Design Centers receive merchandising, visual, and floor display planning assistance, as well as market analysis input.

  • Custom Furniture

    Because we make most of our furniture ourselves, you can offer your clients a robust selection of custom options, from upholstered details to wood finishes and more.

  • Training At Every Turn

    All our designers enjoy product training and hearing the latest tips for maximizing their sales potential. Design Center managers receive additional training, and both groups have access to our online internal support and resource hub, EA Insider.
