Product Details

  • PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

    Our splendid Avon bed balances architectural form with both organic and glam elements. A tall headboard features a carved reed-like pattern, bordered by metal accents to create an eye-catching contrast. A wire-brushed finish brings out the wood's natural grain pattern.

    • Oak bed with a tall headboard that features a carved reeded pattern and metal accents
    • Headboard's fluted pattern is carved in mahogany and accented with decorative metal brackets
    • Available in two sizes: queen and king
    • Requires both a foundation and a mattress
    • Height from floor to side rail: 3.50"; from floor to mattress support slat: 5.75"

  • ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

    • Other materials may be used on internal and secondary components
    • Made in Indonesia to Ethan Allen's standards and specifications
    • Item weight, queen bed: 139 lbs.
    • Item weight, king bed : 161 lbs.
    • Product images may be digitally rendered; final product may vary slightly from rendering.
