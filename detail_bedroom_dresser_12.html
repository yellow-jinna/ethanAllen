Product Details

  • PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

    The Powell double dresser is the utmost in simplicity, it's right for almost any occasion, and it makes any space look amazing. The exterior exudes effortless elegance, but the interior of this solid wood dresser is hardworking, with roomy storage for clothing, bedding, accessories, and more. These dressers are also easy to play up with a few finishing touches, like decorative accents and lighting.

    • Crafted from Indonesian mahogany, random match Indonesian mahogany veneers and ribbon stripe sapele veneers
    • A warm, dark brown stain with black undertones and a high-gloss sheen
    • Nine drawers featuring soft close slides and dovetail joints
    • Ribbon stripe sapele veneers run vertically on drawer fronts
    • Hand applied champagne paint framing drawers
    • Large circular decorative hardware pulls on the six standard drawers and small knobs for the three center drawers all in a champagne brass finish
    • Tapered legs with molding detail
    • A tip-restraint kit is included with this item. It includes safety-tested hardware, a nylon strap, and mounting instructions. Furniture tip-over can cause serious injuries; for your safety, please affix this item securely to the wall

  • ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

    • Made in Indonesia to Ethan Allen's standards and specifications
    • Packaged Weight: 261lbs
    • Product Weight: 225lbs
