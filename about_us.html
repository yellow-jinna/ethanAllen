Iconic Elegance

A Century of Timeless American Style and Craftsmanship

Almost a century of iconic elegance rooted in our vision of American style

A Legacy OfQuality & Innovation

  1. 1932

    The Begining

    Nat Ancell and Ted Baumritter found a housewares sales agency in 1932. They sell plaster gnomes, trellises, and garden swings—all made by someone else.

  2. 1935

    Viva Vermont

    Determined to take control of their destiny, Nat and Ted purchase a bankrupt sawmill for $25,000 and start manufacturing home furnishings. “We fell hopelessly in love with Vermont and with what New England represents historically.“

  3. 1940s

    Innovation Instigated

    No more one-and-done furniture intros; we invent “open stock” furniture styles. Even when some of our furniture is discontinued, new pieces are made in finishes that play well with existing styles.

  4. 1950s

    1,000,000+

    Growing rapidly, we expand our operations to include three sawmills and eleven manufacturing plants. We create our millionth piece of furniture—a beautiful chest crafted from solid maple—and present it to President Eisenhower.represents historically.“

  5. 1960s

    Name It, Claim It

    We officially change our company name to Ethan Allen in 1969, and we begin marketing directly to consumers rather than to furniture retailers.

  6. 1970s

    Deal Me In

    The Ethan Allen Showcase Gallery concept gives independent furniture dealers a recognizable storefront supported by the Ethan Allen name—and brings our complimentary design service front and center.

  7. 1980s

    Bold Moves

    Farooq Kathwari becomes Chairman, President and CEO. To ensure that Ethan Allen can control its own destiny, he leads a management buyout and takes the company private in 1989.

  8. 1990s

    Modern Makeover

    We reinvent our entire furniture line, stepping away from our colonial origins and embracing fresh looks. With our new priorities firmly in place, we once again return to trading on the New York Stock Exchange.

  9. 2000s

    American Brand

    FAs many American manufacturers outsource production internationally, we firmly commit to the skills of our American workers. We invest in new technology and state-of-the-art equipment throughout our manufacturing facilities, continuing to make about 75% of our products in our North American workshops.

  10. 2010s

    For The Future

    We take our place as a world leader in environmentally responsible manufacturing. We also establish our corporate social responsibility vision, ensuring all who work with and for us are treated with respect.

  11. 2020s

    90 Years

    We are named one of America’s Top 10 Retailers by Newsweek, who also named us America’s #1 Premium Furniture retailer for two consecutive years. Ethan Allen designers begin to offer online appointments using our 3D room planner.

Tomorrow

We're Just Getting Started

Ethan Allen is defined by craftsmanship, style, service, quality, and environmental responsibility. We’re also defined by our pioneering and innovative spirit. We’re rooted in our values, but we’re always reaching upward—and the sky’s the limit.
