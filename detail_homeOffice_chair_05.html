Product Details

  • PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

    They say that in order to do good work you have to be flexible; that’s certainly true of Jett, a transitional, pattern-tufted leather desk chair, with a tight back that easily adapts to any work style, with an adjustable base that allows you to sit high or low—and move around all you like. Beyond functional, this armless leather desk chair gets its classic simplicity and no-nonsense good looks from its Parsons roots.

    • A transitional, rolling armless desk chair with tight back and seat and adjustable height
    • Foam and fiber cushion provides a structured seat
    • Engineered hardwood frame for superior strength and long-lasting durability
    • Most of our hand-selected leathers can resist spills and stains.

  • ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

    The dimensions listed are not precise measurements; they have been rounded up to the next whole inch. Our hand-tailoring upholstery involves manually cutting, sewing, and fitting material to frames; the dimensions of each style may vary slightly from piece to piece

    • Interior seating area: 18"d
    • Seat back: 18"h
    • Seat height (floor to top of cushion): 18"
    • Lowest seat height: 18"
    • Highest seat height: 21"
    • Product weight: 46 lbs.

    Construction Features

    • Expertly designed for outstanding comfort; tested for durability and performance
    • The flexible polyurethane foams used in our upholstery are certified through the CertiPUR-US® program
    • Our springs are made from heavy-gauge steel and oven-tempered for a long life and consistent memory
    • Multi-ply engineered hardwood panels with glued and stapled mortise and tenon joints for extraordinary stability
    • Web suspension made of top-quality materials, tested for strength and durability, provides long-lasting constant support
    • Foam and fiber fill provides firm support and minimizes body impressions
    • Product images may be digitally rendered; final product may vary slightly from rendering.
